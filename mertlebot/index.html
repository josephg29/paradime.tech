Build Anything with AI

MertleBot turns your hardware project ideas into complete, buildable designs. Describe what you want to build in plain English, and get wiring diagrams, parts lists, code, and step-by-step instructions.

  • 🤖 Dual AI Engine - Powered by Claude and DeepSeek for optimal results
  • ⚡ All Boards Supported - Arduino, Raspberry Pi, ESP32, and more
  • 📊 Interactive Wiring Diagrams - Visual circuit planning made easy
  • 📦 Complete Parts Lists - With buying links and specifications
  • 💻 Ready-to-Flash Code - Production-ready code for your project
  • 🎯 Skill-Based Guidance - From MONKEY to EXPERT difficulty levels

Originally started as MakeBot in March 2026, MertleBot has evolved into the most comprehensive hardware assistant on the web, helping makers and students bring their electronics ideas to life.
